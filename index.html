Pet the Cat!

How To Play

Pet the Cat: Click on the cat to pet it. Each click counts as one pet.
Random Tolerance: This is the maximum number of times, 1-9, you can pet the cat before it gets angry.
Bank Your Pets: After a few pets, you can click the 'Bank' button to save your score. Banking secures the points you've earned so far.
Take Risks Wisely: You can continue petting the cat after banking, but be careful! If you exceed the cat's tolerance, the game ends.
